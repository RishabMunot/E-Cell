About Us

VJTI, one of the premier engineering institutes in the country is known for the quality of engineers it helps it’s students to be. This premier quality is reflected in start-ups of VJTI. E-Cell VJTI strives to be at the forefront by promoting entrepreneurship as a career, creating holistic and synergistic environment required to inculcate strong entrepreneurial spirit in students.

Our Aim

We at E-CELL VJTI aim to facilitate interaction between individuals with creative minds and ideas with potential for substantial business opportunities and to enable smooth and efficient interaction between its principal components spanning students, faculty, working professionals, aspiring and existing entrepreneurs, mentors, angel investors and venture capitalists.

Our Vision

To create an environment that induces an entrepreneurial mindset to leverage unprecedented advantages by looking beyond conventional ways of doing things and above all, MAKING IDEAS HAPPEN!

Our Initiatives

We at E-Cell, VJTI believe that entrepreneurship is the key to India’s development. To fulfil this vision, we have conceptualised & successfully implemented various initiatives to help students, young entrepreneurs & professionals in their entrepreneurial journey.
